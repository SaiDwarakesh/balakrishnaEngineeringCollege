About BalaKrishna Degree & PG College, Ongole

Established with a commitment to rural empowerment, our college excels in high-quality higher education in the Ongole region. We focus on academic excellence, skill development, and career readiness.

Science Programs

Commerce Programs

Postgraduate Studies

Advanced M.Sc and M.Com courses for students aiming for research and professional roles.
